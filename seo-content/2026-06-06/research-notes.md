- Confusion about whether bathing, feeding and touching are ethical.
- Desire for a practical checklist rather than generic "best sanctuary" lists.
- Need to compare Chiang Mai with Bangkok and Pattaya departures without changing the whole Thailand route.
- Anxiety around hotel pickup, Airbnb addresses, meeting points and Bangkok traffic.
- Family concerns: children, grandparents, walking level, heat, rain and safety instructions.
- Rainy-season uncertainty: cancellation, mud, photos and what to wear.
- Price uncertainty: what inclusions justify the cost and what cheap tours may leave out.
