# Thaielehub SEO/GEO Content Workflow

Use this directory for ongoing content growth work.

## Weekly Output

Each weekly run should create or update a dated folder:

```text
seo-content/YYYY-MM-DD/
```

Include:

- `research-notes.md`: current Reddit, Google, competitor, and AI-search questions.
- `article-backlog.md`: prioritized article opportunities with intent, target page, internal links, and conversion angle.
- `draft-01.md`: one publish-ready original article draft.
- Optional extra drafts when the opportunity is strong.

## Article Requirements

- Pure original English.
- Direct answer near the top.
- Helpful to travelers comparing Chiang Mai, Bangkok, Pattaya, ethical sanctuaries, hotel pickup, half-day versus full-day, families, packing, rainy season, price, and no-riding/no-show questions.
- Include FAQ sections suitable for Google snippets and AI answer engines.
- Use internal links to product pages, pickup-area sections, and related guides.
- Avoid unsupported superlatives. Prefer verifiable, careful language.
- Replace all `Living Green` and `Big Boy` brand references with `Thaielehub` unless the legal company name is specifically required.
